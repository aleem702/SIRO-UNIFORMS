# 🚀 SIRO Uniforms — Simplified Hosting Guide

This guide will get your high-performance demo live on **Vercel** for free. Since your demo uses pre-loaded designs and simulated lead capture, we are using a **Frontend-Only** setup for maximum speed and reliability.

---

## 1. 🚀 Deploy to Vercel
1.  Go to [vercel.com](https://vercel.com/) and create a free "Hobby" account.
2.  Click **"Add New" -> "Project"**.
3.  Connect your GitHub repository: `aleem702/SIRO-UNIFORMS`.
4.  In the configuration screen:
    *   **Framework Preset**: Select "Astro" (should be auto-detected).
    *   **Root Directory**: Click "Edit" and select the `frontend` folder.
5.  Click **Deploy**.

---

## 💡 Why this is better for your demo:
- **Instant Load**: Unlike other free services, Vercel never "sleeps." Your site will load instantly every time the client opens it.
- **Zero Maintenance**: You don't need to manage a database or a backend server for this version of the demo.
- **Museum-Grade Performance**: Vercel's global CDN will ensure your 3D garments and high-res videos play smoothly across the world.

---

## 🔍 Verification
Once deployed, verify your live URL (e.g., `siro-uniforms.vercel.app`) to ensure:
1.  The **Garment Studio** loads all 3D frames correctly.
2.  The **Magazine** flipbook is functional.
3.  All navigation links point to the correct internal pages.
